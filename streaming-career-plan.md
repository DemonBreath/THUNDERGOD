## Phase 1 — Launch sprint (Days 1–30)

*Goal: 12 streams, 5 clips, 1 YouTube video.*

### Stream format (2–3 hr template)

| Block | Duration | Activity |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Warm-up | 10 min | Chat, recap last stream, today’s goal |
| Build | 90–120 min | One focused task (single PR or demo) |
| Show & tell | 15 min | Run the thing; scan QR; click the sky |
| Cooldown | 10 min | Next stream preview, Discord CTA |

**Rule:** one shippable outcome per stream (commit, demo, or doc).

### Discovery habits

- [ ] Clip 1–2 moments per stream (reveal, bug, “it works!”)
- [ ] Post best clip within 24h on X/Bluesky with 1-line context
- [ ] Raid 1–2 streams in Creative / Software tags after each session
- [ ] Add `!discord` and `!github` chat commands

| 2 | First public streams (3×) | 5 unique chatters total |

| 4 | First YouTube upload (edited VOD) | 10 followers |
